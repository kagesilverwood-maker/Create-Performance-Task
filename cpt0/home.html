About Sailing

Sailing is an activity that involves using the wind to power your boat. This is done by adjusting sails, steering the boat, and optimizing use of the wind. It can be competitive, recreational, or a way to travel. Sailing is also one of the only sports you can do throughout your whole life and is accessible for everyone.

Learn More

Common Types of Sailboats

  • Dinghy- Small sailboat, typically under 15-20 feet and can be sailed by 1-3 people.
  • Keelboat- Medium sized sailboat that has a permanent fixed keel. They are generally very stable and designed for offshore sailing.
  • Catamaran and Trimaran- Have more than one hull and are known for being really fast and stable.
  • Yacht and other cruising sailboat- Large sailboats that are designed to be taken on long voyages. They are usually over 25 feet and require a large crew.

Features

Sailing involves adjusting sails, steering, and using wind for propulsion.
